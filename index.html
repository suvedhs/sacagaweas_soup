About

Sacagawea's Soup is a webapp created by 4 hackers at HackUTA on a mission to improve the lives of those who can't cook. You input your kitchen inventory and we give recipes you can work with.

Whether you're a student looking to make a free meal, a professional looking to expand horizons, or a food enthusiast who enjoys staring at recipes, this application is the perfect fit!
